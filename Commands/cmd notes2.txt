2. Train from scratch or transfer learning
You can train your own YOLOv5n / YOLOv5s / YOLOv5m from scratch using only your dataset.
Weights: Don’t use the official pretrained .pt files.
You may lose some accuracy, but with 3,500 labeled images, fine-tuning from your own pretrained models (or very small “unlicensed” initializations) can still work well.

python train.py --img 640 --batch 16 --epochs 100 --data railroad-cars/data.yaml --weights "" --name railroad_detector
python train.py --img 640 --batch 16 --epochs 100 --data railroad-cars/data.yaml --name railroad_detector_scratch

python train.py --img 640 --batch 512 --epochs 100 --data railroad-cars/data.yaml --cfg models/yolov5x.yaml --name railroad_detector_teacher --device 0,1,2,3,4,5,6,7 --cache ram --half
